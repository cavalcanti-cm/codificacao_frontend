Simples Omelette Recipe


An easy quick dish, perfect for amy meal. This classic omelette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choice of cheese vegetabables, or meats.

Preparation time


  • TotalAproximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation 5 minutes
  • Cooking 5 minutes

Ingredients


  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt, to taste
  • Pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter ar oil
  • Optional filings: cheese, diced vegetabables, cooked meats, herbs
